About

Welcome to ABC Optics!

ABC Optics is one of the well-known optician in Brisbane. ABC Optics was etablished in 2013 and has treated many patients since then. We have been introducing more services to make our patients satisfied as many as possible.

We provide ophthalmic glasses, sunglasses, and contact lenses as well as excellent services for children's needs with professional caring approach. We are well regarded locally for many optical services but especially for optical requirements of children.

Our Services

  • Eye examinations*
  • Spectacles**
  • Diabetic eye examinations
  • Colour vision assessment
  • Macular degeneration test
  • Glaucoma & eye pressure testing
  • Checks for cataracts
  • Cleaness of vision***
  • Focusing ability****
  • Eye muscle movement and control check
  • Ambltopia (laze eye)
  • Contact lens fitting and management
  • Referrals for eye laser surgery
  • Mining & industrial safety spectacles
  • Advice on your correct fitting of good quality sunglasses*****

Our Staffs

Our friendly staffs are always ready for your matters. We have two qualified optometrists at our store and they are ready to diagnose and treat a number of eye related problems.

Our optometrists strive to make a positive difference to the health and well-being of our customers, endeavouring to obtain the best visual outcomes so that our customers realise their full potential in work, study, sport and leisure.
